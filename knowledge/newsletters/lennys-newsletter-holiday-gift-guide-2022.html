Out and about

  1. Goodr sunglasses: Discovered via @jess and now my favorite sunglasses. Affordable, polarized, great for sports, and there are dozens of styles. Also check out Izipizi.

  1. Herschel Tech Daypack: I was gifted this earlier this year, and I’ve never come across a more well-thought-through laptop backpack. Makes you want to travel more.

  1. JBL Charge 5 Portable Bluetooth Speaker: Best portable Bluetooth speaker I’ve owned. People also like these Wonderbooms.

  2. AllTrails Pro subscription: A thoughtful gift for your outdoorsy friends. I’ve been a paid subscriber for 5+ years now. Inspired by@skonomics2k.

Home

  1. ZWILLING Personal Smoothie Blender: My wife got us one of these a few months ago, and we’re now making shakes almost every day. Many people also love the Nutribullet.

  1. Kyocera 8” Ceramic Chef’s Knife: Ceramic knives are sharper, lighter, dishwasher-safe, and stay sharp for longer than steel knives. How did I just learn about this? My wife got us these after watching a lot of Emily Mariko.

  1. Brightland olive oil: Often recommended by my foodie friends.

  1. Bierfilzl Merino Wool Felt Square Coaster: The one coaster in our home I find myself actively seeking out when I sit down for a cup of tea.

  2. Dlyfull Universal Battery Tester: Thanks to this thing, I throw away so many fewer batteries now, knowing which are dead and which are still just fine. Works with every battery type.
